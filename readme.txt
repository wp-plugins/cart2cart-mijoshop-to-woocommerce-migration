== Description ==

Cart2Cart: MijoShop to WooCommerce Migration plugin will make your platform switch as comfortable as possible. It offers an exceptional possibility to perform an accurate data transfer with no programing skills required. Everything you have to do is to establish a connection between your stores, choose the entities you want to transfer and adjust the migration to your needs with the help of additional options. Afterwards, try our free Demo Migration or just launch Full Migration at once and Cart2Cart will handle the transfer on its own! Please not, the plugin itself is free to download, but migration is paid and starts from $69.

= Features =
* **Free Demo Migration.** Put into test Cart2Cart plugin by performing Demo Migration absolutely for free before proceeding with the actual switch. 
* **Qualified Support.** Contact Cart2Cart Support Team and get professional assistance concerning any question you have.
* **100% Uptime.** Your current MijoShop cart is guaranteed to stay online during the whole migration process. 
* **Reasonable Price Policy.** Pay only for what you get - migration cost fully depends on the amount of transferring entities and additional options chosen.

**Transfer the following data from MijoShop to WooCommerce:**

* Products, product images, product extra fields, product attributes;
* Categories, category images;
* Customers, customer shipping address, customer billing address;
* Orders, order statuses, order products;
* Manufacturers, taxes, reviews.

*Supported WooCommerce versions:* 1.1.x - 1.6.x, 2.x
**Please note:** Cart2Cart migrates data only. You current MijoShop design won’t be transferred. 

Before the migration, you need to make a few simple steps. At first, download the migration plugin, activate it and get your MijoShop and WooCommerce stores connected via Connection Bridge. Then you’ll be redirected to Cart2Cart website in order to proceed with the migration. To make sure everything is set properly, choose free Demo Migration option, that will move up to 10 entities to WooCommerce. If you’re ready to start, select Insurance plan (not necessary) and launch Full Migration. Now everything is in hands of Cart2Cart.

**Pre-Migration Steps**

1. Install WooCommerce store and make sure it can be reached via internet.
1. Check for FTP details for your shopping carts. You need them to perform migration.

== Installation ==

1. Download plugin.zip file and extract it to your hard drive
1. Upload unzipped file to wp-content/plugin directory
1. Activate “Cart2Cart MijoShop to WooCommerce Migration” in plugins section of your admin panel
1. Insert FTP details to upload the Connection Bridge files for MijoShop
1. Connection Bridge files for WooCommerce are will be uploaded automatically
1. Click Start Migration button and follow the Migration Wizard to perform accurate and swift migration

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Can I migrate my reviews to WooCommerce? =
Cart2Cart service supports the transfers of reviews from and to the WooCommerce shopping cart. Additionally, merchants will be presented with opportunity to move review ratings.

= Does Cart2Cart service migrate taxes? =
Yes, taxes migration is available within our service. Note that, due to WooCommerce peculiarities, they will be already included to product prices after the migration.

= Is it possible to migrate to the same domain on a different directory? =
Yes, it is. You can migrate within same domain using different folders.
